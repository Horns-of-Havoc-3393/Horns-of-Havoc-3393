How to Donate

Step 1: Go to the Official FIRST Washington Donation website.

Step 2: Select "FRC 3393" from the "Purpose" dropdown menu as shown in the image

Step 3: Enter the amount you wish to donate.

Step 4: Fill in your personal information and payment details.

Step 5: Click "Donate" to complete your donation.
